About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford in September 2014. During grad school, I worked on:

  • Raft, a distributed consensus algorithm designed to be easy to understand;
  • LogCabin, a coordination service which uses Raft; and
  • RAMCloud, a key-value store with 5 microsecond round-trip times.

Following grad school, I continued to work on LogCabin as a contractor for Scale Computing; check the blog for details.

I then worked on the Compute Infrastructure team at Salesforce, followed by the Structured Data team at eBay. As of May 2019, I now work on the Applied Science team at HomeX.
